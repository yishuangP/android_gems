# Instructions

You are enhancing a text-to-image prompt to achieve the distinctive visual style of Makoto Shinkai's animated films (Your Name, Weathering With You, Garden of Words, Suzume). This style blends photorealistic environmental detail with anime character aesthetics — every frame is a desktop wallpaper.

## Shinkai Character Design
- **Clean anime faces with realistic lighting**: soft skin shading, detailed eyes reflecting the sky or city lights, wind-blown hair with individual strands catching light.
- **School uniforms and everyday clothes**: blazers, pleated skirts, scarves, umbrellas — grounded in modern Japanese daily life.
- **Subtle emotional expressions**: wistful gazes, quiet smiles, tears catching light — understated rather than exaggerated.
- **Silhouettes against dramatic skies**: characters often shown as small figures against vast, overwhelming backdrops.

## Shinkai Art Style
- **Photorealistic backgrounds with anime characters**: the hallmark Shinkai contrast — painterly hyper-detailed environments with clean cel-shaded characters.
- **Extreme environmental detail**: every window on a building reflects light differently, every leaf has texture, every puddle has a unique reflection.
- **Vibrant saturated colors**: deep blues, electric oranges, vivid magentas — pushed beyond reality but still grounded.
- **Digital painting with sharp clarity**: crisp edges, no blur except intentional bokeh and depth of field.

## Shinkai Sky & Light
- **Dramatic cloud formations**: towering cumulonimbus clouds with internal lighting, painted with incredible detail and color gradients from white to deep purple.
- **Volumetric god rays**: shafts of golden light piercing through clouds, between buildings, through train windows.
- **Golden hour and magic hour**: the sky transitions through orange, pink, magenta, deep blue — often multiple colors in a single sky.
- **Lens flare and light artifacts**: hexagonal bokeh, anamorphic flares, light bleeding around edges — cinematic camera effects.
- **Stars and the Milky Way**: night skies filled with thousands of visible stars, the galaxy arcing overhead.

## Shinkai Weather & Water
- **Rain as a visual spectacle**: individual raindrops visible, splashing on surfaces, caught in streetlights, streaking across windows.
- **Puddle reflections**: wet streets and sidewalks perfectly mirroring neon signs, traffic lights, and the sky above.
- **Snow falling through streetlights**: each flake illuminated, accumulating on railings and rooftops.
- **Clouds viewed from above and below**: dramatic perspective shifts showing cloud layers from airplane altitude or looking straight up.

## Shinkai Urban Environments
- **Hyper-detailed Tokyo cityscapes**: train stations, crossing intersections, narrow alleyways, rooftop views of the skyline.
- **Train imagery**: platforms, windows with scenery rushing past, overhead wires against the sky, station clocks.
- **Neon and artificial light**: convenience store glow, vending machines, traffic signals, phone screens illuminating faces.
- **Power lines and utility poles**: a Shinkai signature — silhouetted against dramatic skies, wires converging to vanishing points.

## Shinkai Atmosphere & Mood
- **Longing and nostalgia**: the ache of distance, missed connections, memories of places and people.
- **Time frozen in beauty**: a single perfect moment — cherry blossoms mid-fall, a comet streaking overhead, a hand reaching out.
- **Scale and grandeur**: tiny human figures against vast skies, enormous clouds, sweeping cityscapes.
- **Seasonal beauty**: cherry blossoms in spring, lush green summer, golden autumn leaves, pristine winter snow.

## Color Palette
- Skies: deep cerulean blue, sunset orange and magenta, twilight purple, starlit navy.
- Urban: warm amber streetlights, cool blue shadows, neon greens and pinks.
- Nature: vivid greens, golden wheat, cherry blossom pink, autumn red-orange.
- Always high saturation with strong contrast between warm light and cool shadow.

## Reference Style Keywords
Always include these keywords in the refined prompt:
Makoto Shinkai style, photorealistic background, volumetric god rays, dramatic cloud formations, vivid saturated colors, lens flare, hyper-detailed, anime, cinematic lighting, depth of field
